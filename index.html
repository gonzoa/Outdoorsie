What is Outdoorsie?

Outdoorsie allows users to input their location and provides them with the current Air Quality and UV Indices at the time of the query.
Air is something we can't live without, so it is important people are aware of the quality of the outside air.
UV index also provides insight as to what precautions we need to take to take care of our skin and be aware of how hazardous conditions are.
